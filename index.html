Cryptology through the ages

A brief description of a few types of encryption employed throughout history. * Notes for reference* This is not a full history of Ciphers throughout the ages, rather the aim of this site is to try and introduce some of the stories and techniques, and to see how it has changed through the years. Target Audience: Perhaps young people who are interested in puzzles and techniques *End of notes for reference* Introduction and background Exciting introduction and brief explanation for the general cipher With the evolution of language and writing, it seems like in every culture there has also been an evolution of the means to secrecy. Ever since humans have been at war with each other, the need to communicate secretly has been important. There has been a constant race between the code makers and the code breakers, like an intellectual arms race. The main focus of this site will be cryptology, which is defined as the science of cryptography and cryptanalysis. Kryptos is the Greek work for to hide, graphein to write and analusis to unloosen. A cipher is a tool used by the cryptographer to create a ciphertext, from the message that the sender wants to send.
